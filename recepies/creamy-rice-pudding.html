Creamy Rice Pudding

This creamy rice pudding is a comforting dessert that's easy to make and delicious to eat. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a rich and satisfying treat that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're looking for a sweet ending to a meal or a cozy snack, this rice pudding is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients You'll Need

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)

How to Make Creamy Rice Pudding Step-By-Step

  1. Rinse the Arborio rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. In a large saucepan, combine the rice, whole milk, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and salt. Stir to combine.
  3. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low. Simmer, stirring frequently, until the rice is tender and the pudding has thickened, about 25-30 minutes. If using raisins, stir them in during the last 5 minutes of cooking.
  4. Once the rice pudding has thickened to your desired consistency, remove it from the heat. Let it cool slightly before serving.
  5. Serve the rice pudding warm or chilled, garnished with a sprinkle of cinnamon or a dollop of whipped cream if desired.
